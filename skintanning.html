Why do we need treatments for tan removal?

UnTan or treatments for tan removal are essential for maintaining healthy, radiant skin, correcting sun damage, and enhancing overall skin appearance and confidence. They are a vital part of any comprehensive skincare routine, especially for those who spend a lot of time outdoors. At Uncover Clinic, we offer a comprehensive range of treatments designed to reduce tanning, enhance skin brightness, and restore an even skin tone.

Concern Areas

  • Hyperpigmentation
  • Sunspots
  • Uneven Skin Tone
  • Melasma
  • Freckles
  • Dull and Lackluster Skin

What are the effective treatments for Sun Tan Removal?

Our medi facials and chemical peels, specially known as the UnTan Glow Peels are designed to exfoliate the skin, remove dead skin cells, and promote cell regeneration. These treatments help to lighten pigmentation, reduce tanning, and improve skin texture. Depending upon the skin type we choose from the following

  • Glycolic Peel: Targets fine lines, dullness, and uneven texture.
  • Salicylic Peel: Effective for acne-prone skin and reducing dark spots.
  • Lactic Peel: Gentle peel for sensitive skin, promoting hydration and brightness.

  • Laser Toning: Laser toning is a non-invasive treatment that uses laser technology to target pigmentation and stimulate collagen production. This helps in reducing dark spots, improving skin tone, and enhancing overall skin brightness.
    • ‍
  • IV Glow Drips: Glow drips are infused with vitamins, minerals and special ingredients like Glutathione, that help in brightening the skin overall.
  • Technology Q-Switch Laser: The Q-Switch Laser is a state-of-the-art technology used in our skin tanning and brightening treatments. It emits high-intensity laser beams that penetrate the skin to break down pigmentation and stimulate collagen production. This technology is highly effective in treating various pigmentation issues with minimal discomfort and downtime.
